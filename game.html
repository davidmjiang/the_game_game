The Game

Score: 0

Cards Left: 98

Goal

The goal of The Game is to play as many cards as possible onto the four piles.

Setup

The deck has 98 cards, one for each number between 2 and 99. You start by drawing 7 cards into your hand. Each turn, you must play at least 2 cards from your hand before drawing back to 7. Select a card from your hand by clicking on it and then click "Add to Pile" to add your card to your chosen pile. After you have played 2 cards, you may draw cards by clicking "Draw Cards". Each pile shows the top card in that pile. To see all the cards that have been played, click "See all piles".

The Piles

Two piles start from 1 and go up to 99. You may play a card on these piles only if it is larger than the top card of the pile.

Two piles start from 100 and go down to 2. You may play a card on these piles only if is smaller than the top card of the pile.

Jumping

In addition to the rules above, you may also play a "jump". For the piles going down, you may play a card EXACTLY 10 more than the top card. For the pile going up, you may play a card EXACTLY 10 less than the top card. For example, on a piles that is going down with a top card of 55, you may play 65. The game continues as normal, playing cards lower than 65.

End of Game

The game ends if you can no longer make a legal play from the cards in your hand.

Good luck!
